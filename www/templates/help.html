How Casher Works

  1. 1. Click on the type of bill you want and how much you want. (i.e. If you want five $1 bills click the 1 button 5 times.
  2. 2. Casher adds up the amount of bills you want
  3. 3. Click the button below that says "Get Cash".
  4. 4. Your atm comes to you and gives you the cash.
  5. 5. You pay the ATM via venmo.
  6. 6. Part ways!
